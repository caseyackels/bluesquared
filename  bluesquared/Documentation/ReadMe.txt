ListDisplay 0.3.6

Usage: To determine the amount of boxes per destination
This puts you on the fasttrack to calculating how many boxes per destination you will have.
Manually calculating this is no more. Now you can get your work done twice as fast.

Tips:
Change fields with the <Tab> key.
Use the Enter key to insert your desired destination quantities, instead of using the "Add to List" button.
Hit the Escape to close the window.
Select an entry and press the Delete key to remove an entry.
